LVSN


It's 2018 a warm May evening in Sweden, the 20-year-old Linus Silfverlood and 22-year-old Viktor Nuorimaa has just created the electronic music group LVSN. Now, several years later LVSN has accumulated over 10M streams worldwide. They have proven themselves as the versatile and creative producers that they truly are. A mixture of music containing lyrics that can form a deep connection with the listener, to the upbeat songs that will capture your heart while dancing the night away. With their exceptional array of different genres as seen in their music, LVSN has built an impressive music catalog including singles such as “Stolen Kisses” ft. ZANA and “Eyes on You” together with Lü, Cat Mode and The High. Their single “Blue Lagoon” with FIXL ft. Finn Kleffmann has accumulated over 2M streams. This is just the beginning, and the story continues...
